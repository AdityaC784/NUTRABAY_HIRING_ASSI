Job Title: Digital Marketing Analyst — Nutrabay

About Nutrabay:
Nutrabay is India's largest health and nutrition platform. We sell 5000+ products across sports nutrition, vitamins, and wellness.

Role Overview:
We are looking for a Digital Marketing Analyst to join our growth team. The ideal candidate will analyze campaign performance, manage paid ads, and drive ROI across channels.

Key Responsibilities:
- Manage and optimize Google Ads, Meta Ads, and affiliate campaigns
- Analyze performance data using Google Analytics and Excel/Sheets
- Build weekly performance reports and present insights to stakeholders
- Conduct A/B tests on landing pages and ad creatives
- Coordinate with content and design teams for campaign assets

Required Skills:
- 1-3 years of experience in digital marketing or growth roles
- Strong knowledge of Google Ads, Meta Business Manager
- Proficiency in Excel / Google Sheets (pivot tables, VLOOKUP)
- Google Analytics / GA4 experience
- Strong analytical and communication skills

Good to Have:
- Experience with e-commerce or D2C brands
- Knowledge of SEO/SEM basics
- Familiarity with tools like SEMrush, Ahrefs, or Looker Studio

Education:
- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or related field

Location: Delhi / Remote
Type: Full-time
